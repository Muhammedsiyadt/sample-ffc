What is Permanent Placement ?

At Nursing Pool Australia, we specialize in arranging permanent placement for nursing jobs around the globe. Whether you're looking for a new challenge or seeking to advance your nursing career, we're here to help you find the perfect permanent nursing job that meets your specific needs and preferences.

Our expert team of recruiters works closely with healthcare employers to match you with the best opportunities in the nursing field. With our commitment to excellence, we're dedicated to helping you arrange permanent placements in Queensland, New South wales, Tasmania, Victoria, Northern Territory, and more!

Sponsorship

About Sponsorships

At Nursing Pool Australia, we make it easy for nurses to work abroad by providing Visa Sponsorship and AHPRA (Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency) registration assistance. We understand that navigating the visa and registration processes can be complicated and time-consuming, which is why we offer comprehensive support to help you through every step of the way.

Whether you're a nurse seeking new opportunities in Australia or an employer looking to sponsor international nursing staff, we've got you covered. With our expert guidance and commitment to excellence, we're here to make your nursing career dreams a reality.
